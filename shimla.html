About Shimla

Shimla is the capital of Himachal Pradesh and one of the most famous tourist destinations in northern India. Surrounded by beautiful hills and forests, Shimla offers breathtaking views, snowfall in winter, shopping streets, cafes, and peaceful nature experiences.

Top Places In Shimla

Mall Road

Mall Road is Shimla's most famous shopping and tourist street filled with cafes, restaurants, and beautiful mountain views.

Kufri

Kufri is known for snowfall, adventure sports, horse riding, and scenic Himalayan landscapes.

Jakhoo Temple

Jakhoo Temple is a famous hilltop temple offering panoramic views of Shimla city.

Famous Food Of Shimla

Shimla is famous for Himachali dishes like Siddu, Chha Gosht, Madra, and traditional mountain cuisine. Cafes in Shimla also serve delicious snacks, coffee, and bakery items with beautiful hill views.

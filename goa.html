About Goa

Goa is located on the western coast of India along the Arabian Sea. It is famous for beaches like Baga, Calangute, Anjuna, Vagator, and Palolem. Goa is also known for its nightlife, churches, forts, water sports, and delicious seafood. Every year millions of tourists visit Goa for relaxation, parties, adventure, and honeymoon trips.

Top Places In Goa

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is one of Goa's most famous beaches known for nightlife, beach shacks, water sports, and music parties.

Fort Aguada

Fort Aguada is a beautiful Portuguese fort offering amazing sea views and historical architecture.

Palolem Beach

Palolem Beach is famous for peaceful vibes, clean water, and beautiful palm tree landscapes.

Famous Food Of Goa

Goa is famous for seafood dishes like fish curry rice, prawn curry, crab masala, bebinca dessert, and Goan vindaloo. Beach cafes and restaurants serve delicious international and Indian cuisine with sea views.

About Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Banaras, is located on the banks of the River Ganga. It is one of India's most important spiritual destinations and attracts millions of pilgrims and tourists every year. The city is known for temples, ancient streets, classical music, and evening Ganga Aarti.

Top Places In Varanasi

Dashashwamedh Ghat

Dashashwamedh Ghat is famous for the spectacular evening Ganga Aarti ceremony.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Assi Ghat

Assi Ghat is popular for sunrise views, boat rides, yoga sessions, and peaceful atmosphere.

Famous Food Of Varanasi

Varanasi is famous for Banarasi Paan, Kachori Sabzi, Malaiyyo, Lassi, Chaat, and traditional sweets. The city's street food is loved by tourists from all over India.

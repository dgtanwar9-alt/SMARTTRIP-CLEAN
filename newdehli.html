About New Delhi

New Delhi is one of the most visited cities in India and serves as the political and cultural heart of the country. The city is known for historical monuments, luxury hotels, shopping markets, museums, nightlife, and delicious food. Delhi combines Mughal architecture with modern urban life.

Top Places In Delhi

India Gate

India Gate is one of the most iconic landmarks of Delhi and a popular tourist attraction.

Red Fort

The Red Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for Mughal architecture and history.

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is one of the tallest historical towers in India and a famous tourist place.

Famous Food Of Delhi

Delhi is famous for street foods like Chole Bhature, Momos, Kebabs, Butter Chicken, Parathas, and Chandni Chowk sweets. The city is one of the best food destinations in India.
